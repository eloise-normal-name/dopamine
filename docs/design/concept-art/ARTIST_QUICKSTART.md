# Artist Quickstart

Get from zero to concept art in five minutes.

## What You're Making

Visual concepts for **"Neon Night Market Slots"** ‚Äî a cozy, neon-lit slot machine game that feels like finding your favorite late-night arcade. Think vaporwave-meets-retro-arcade: purple gradients, warm neon glow, satisfying mechanical rhythm.

## The Prompt-Based Method

Instead of drawing from scratch, you'll:

1. **Read a creative prompt** from our prompt library
2. **Feed it to an AI image tool** (DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion)
3. **Generate several variations** ‚Äî run it 5-10 times
4. **Pick the ones that feel right** ‚Äî trust your gut
5. **Iterate** ‚Äî tweak the prompt, regenerate, refine

The prompts are written to be evocative and open-ended. They describe moods and feelings, not pixel-perfect specifications. This is intentional ‚Äî we want the AI to surprise us.

## Your First Five Minutes

1. Open [`slot-machine/AI_IMAGE_GENERATION_PROMPTS.md`](slot-machine/AI_IMAGE_GENERATION_PROMPTS.md)
2. Scroll to **Direction 02: Neon Night Market Environment** (best starting point)
3. Copy the "Living Night Market" prompt
4. Paste it into your AI image tool
5. Generate 4-5 variations
6. Save anything that sparks joy in `slot-machine/environment/`

That's it. You're doing concept art.

## What "Good" Looks Like

A successful concept:
- ‚úÖ Makes you feel something (cozy? excited? curious?)
- ‚úÖ Captures the "neon night market" mood
- ‚úÖ Could inspire specific game assets
- ‚úÖ Surprises you in a good way

A concept doesn't need to be:
- ‚ùå Technically precise
- ‚ùå Production-ready
- ‚ùå Exactly what anyone expected

## Workflow for a Full Exploration

**Days 1-2**: Pick a direction, generate 15+ variations, save everything interesting
**Days 3-4**: Narrow to 3-5 favorites, refine prompts based on what worked, generate focused variations
**Day 5**: Select 1-2 strongest concepts, document what makes them work

Then move to the next direction (Environment ‚Üí Celebration ‚Üí Machine Details).

## AI Tool Tips

**DALL-E**: Interprets descriptive language well. Write prompts like you're telling a story.
**Midjourney**: Strong artistic interpretation. Use `--stylize` for more creative latitude. Embrace unexpected results.
**Stable Diffusion**: Fine control available. Use negative prompts to steer away from unwanted elements without over-constraining.
